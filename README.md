The world outside dissolves into the grey,
A quiet room where time begins to slow.
No sudden lights to pull the mind away,
Just perfect stillness, stepping into flow.

Between the sudden spark and written word,
A silent partner waits in deep repose.
Where every whispered, half-formed thought is heard,
And softly blooms, as natural as a rose.

Where Automation lets the heavy fall.
A Client answering with gentle grace,
With Tact to seamlessly connect it all.

A quiet river moving toward the sea,
Where conscious effort and the act are one.
The beautiful illusion you are free,
As thought translates to magic, softly done.
